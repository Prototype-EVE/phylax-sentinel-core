## 3. Monitoring & Accountability Protocol
To address concerns regarding the monitoring of "Offline-First" systems:
* **Pseudonymous Officer Logging:** The system generates a cryptographic "Math Receipt" for every transaction. While subject data is purged in <0.5s, the **Officer's** identity and metadata are logged to a tamper-proof ledger for accountability.
* **Hardware Trust Binding:** Every instance of the Sentinel Core utilizes a unique Hardware Root of Trust (RoT), preventing the execution of unauthorized or "shadow" firmware.
